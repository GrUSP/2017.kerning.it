Davide Mottes
@davidemottes

He was born and raised in the heart of the Trentino’s Alps. From 2005 he lives in Milan where he graduated from Politecnico di Milano in Communication Design. In the last seven years he has been working as editorial designer for the newsroom of IL, the monthly magazine of Il Sole 24 ORE. He is teaching assistant at Politecnico di Milano and Università Iuav di Venezia. Davide is passionate about typography, publishing and infographic, specially in cartography. He’s never been to Switzerland, but he loves the Swiss style, most of all the graphic design.

Anatomy of a Magazine

A practical guide to design your own magazine.

For two days you will be an editorial designer and you will oversee the whole creative process of a magazine, from the idea to the printing files.

{% if page.short %}

Read more

{% else %}

In the first part of the workshop you will focus on understanding the structure, defying format, different sections and rhythm of your magazine.

Then you will design several covers and the typographic grid, an essential aim to combine texts, photos and illustrations into different layouts. And of course, you will discover how the typography matters.
